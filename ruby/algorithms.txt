Elevator algorithm
Robot will store desired floor in variable. 
Stand in front of doors until they open, if people are not exiting, enter elevator. Find control panel. If floor variable has not been selected, press number. When elevator reaches desired floor, exit.

Gym workout
Workout will be stored in dictionary, exercise => reps.
For each exercise in dict
    initialize counter 
    until counter is equal to reps 
        perform exercise 
        increment counter by one 
        When counter is equal to reps
        move on to next exercise
